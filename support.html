Support

Email: oguzhanxsatilmis@gmail.com

When contacting us, please include:

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iOS device to manage or cancel.

Q: How do I request a refund?

A: Refunds are processed by Apple, not by us directly. Please visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

Q: I found a bug, what should I do?

A: Please email us with details about the issue, including steps to reproduce it, and we'll investigate as soon as possible.
