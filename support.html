Frequently Asked Questions

The app crashes or freezes

Try restarting the app and make sure it's updated to the latest version from the App Store. If the issue continues, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

I have a feature request

We'd love to hear it. Send your idea through the contact form and we'll consider it for a future update.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request one.

Still need help?

Reach out via the Contact page and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
