Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how Oğuzhan Satılmış ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with the mobile applications ("Apps") we publish on the Apple App Store. By downloading or using any of our Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Our Apps are designed to collect as little information as possible. Depending on the App, the following limited information may be processed:

  • Diagnostic and crash data used to identify and fix bugs.
  • Basic, non-identifying usage statistics (such as feature usage) to help us improve the App.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us for support, such as your email address.

We do not require account creation, and we do not collect sensitive personal information such as precise location, contacts, or health data unless a specific App explicitly requests it for a core feature, in which case this is disclosed to you at the time of the request.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our Apps;
  • Diagnose technical issues and fix bugs;
  • Respond to your support requests and communications.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps may rely on third-party services (for example, Apple's own frameworks for crash reporting or analytics) to help us understand App stability and performance. These providers may process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We do not share your information with third parties, except where required to provide App functionality, comply with applicable law, or protect our legal rights.

Data Storage & Security

Where information is stored, we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. Any data stored locally on your device remains under your control and can be removed by deleting the App.

Data Retention

We retain any collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, or to request that it be corrected or deleted, subject to applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our Apps or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
